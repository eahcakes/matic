About Sta. Maria De Matthias Center Orphanage

Sta. Maria De Matthias Orphanage Center is a sanctuary for boys and girls of all ages located in Marikina City, Philippines. The orphanage provides a safe and nurturing environment for children who have lost their families or are in need of care.

Address: 112 IPIL STREET, MARIKINA HEIGHTS 1810 MARIKINA CITY

Our Goal

What We Aim to Achieve

Our primary goal is to bring joy, support, and hope to the children of Sta. Maria De Matthias Orphanage. We aim to provide them with essential items, emotional support, and memorable experiences.

How We Plan to Do It

Through donations of gently used toys, clothes, and other necessities, as well as organizing fun activities, we strive to make a positive impact on their lives.

Our Plans

For our project, we aim to bring joy and support to the children of Sta. Maria De Matthias Orphanage through donations and meaningful interactions.

Our Plan

  • Donate gently used toys, clothes, and essential items.
  • Organize fun activities and games to entertain the children.
  • Spend quality time with the kids to provide emotional support and companionship.
